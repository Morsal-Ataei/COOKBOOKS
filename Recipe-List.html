Chicken-Biryani

Afghani, delicious and Spicy Food.
Chicken-Biryani, mixed peppers & onion mixed with rice.
Biryani is a mixed rice dish popular in South Asia, made with rice, meat or seafood, and spices.
To cater to vegetarians, the meat or seafood can be substituted with vegetables or paneer. Sometimes eggs or potatoes are also added.
